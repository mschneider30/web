Touchdown

  • Worth 7 points
      This makes it the most valuable score in football. This is what a team's offense strives for throughout the game.
  • Scored by entering the endzone
      As shown below, this is done not by a players body entering the endzone, but by the ball entering.
  • Can be scored by running in or passing in
      The picture above shows a player running in the ball. However a touchdown can also be scored by a player cating the ball in the endzone, as shown below.
  • Most Common Method
      This is more common than any other score in football. This is due to it being the offense's first priority.
  • Usually scored by offense, but can be achieved on defense after a interception or a fumble
      If the defense intercepts a pass, or recovers a fumble, it is allowed to run the ball ito the endzone. If this is done they earn their team 7 points. However, this is quite rare and usually doesn't happen.
